Common Topics

Subscriptions and purchases

  • Manage or cancel a subscription: Open the Google Play app > tap your profile icon > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions.
  • Restore previous purchases: Make sure you are signed in with the same Google Account used for the purchase, then reopen FitWoW and check your membership or credit balance.
  • Refund requests: Refunds are handled by Google Play. Visit Google Play order history or Google Play Help.
  • Workout-credit pass not showing: Make sure you are signed in with the same Google Account used to purchase. If still missing, email us with your Google Play order ID.

Account

  • Sign in options: Sign in with Google. Use the same Google account each time to access the same FitWoW account.
  • Delete your account: In the app, go to Settings > Account Management > Delete Account. This permanently deletes your account and associated data.
  • Sign out: Settings > Account Management > Sign Out.

Recording and AI analysis

  • Camera permission denied: Open Android Settings > Apps > FitWoW > Permissions > Camera, then allow camera access and return to the app.
  • Analysis taking a long time: Workout video is uploaded after each set. On slow networks this can take a few minutes per session. The report appears once analysis completes.
  • AI rep count seems off: Open the report and tap any exercise to correct the count manually before saving.

Reports and history

  • Find past sessions: Settings > My Reports.
  • Delete a session: In My Reports, swipe left on a card and tap Delete.
  • Share a report: Open the report and tap the Share icon.

Bug Reports and Feedback

For bug reports or product feedback, email us at fitwow2026@gmail.com. The more detail (steps to reproduce, screenshots, video) the faster we can fix it.
